Featured Historic Sites

Handpicked sites that showcase Morocco's rich history and architecture.

Ait Benhaddou

UNESCO ksar and cinematic landmark on the old caravan route to the Sahara.

Marrakech Medina

Vibrant souks, Bahia Palace, and the famous Jemaa el-Fnaa square.

Fes Tanneries

Ancient medina with traditional leather tanneries and historic madrasas.

Chefchaouen

The iconic blue city tucked in the Rif mountains — photography heaven.

Hassan II Mosque

Imposing ocean-front mosque in Casablanca with intricate tilework.

Volubilis

Roman ruins near Meknes with well-preserved mosaics and architecture.
